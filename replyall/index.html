Reply all

Nobody likes reading emails, but there’s nothing worse than having to scroll through a list of emails meant for one person but sent to everyone because someone hit reply all instead of reply. Let’s fix this.

Learn more »

Outlook app

iOS and Android

Unfortunately, Microsoft don’t think this is a problem on mobile devices. You just need to be careful when replying that you click the icon on the left and then ‘reply’. Do not click the text box at the bottom.

Outlook website

outlook.office365.com

We need to change a setting on the website. Click the settings cog in the top-right corner, search for ‘reply’, select ‘reply options’ and change your default to ‘reply’. Simples.
